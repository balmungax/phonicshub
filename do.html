When to use Do and Does?

Learn the rule, then test yourself!

do
Use with
I You We They
👥 More than one person, or talking about yourself / the person you speak to
does
Use with
He She It
👤 One other person (boy/girl) or one animal/thing
💡 Easy trick: He / She / It → always does. Everyone else → do.
